About the Exhibition

Engineering the Future in Qatar

The Mechatronics Engineering programme at Qatar University integrates mechanical, electrical, and software disciplines. This exhibition showcases year-long capstone efforts addressing real engineering challenges across robotics, biomedical, energy, and autonomous systems.

Each team spent the academic year designing, building, and validating their solutions — these projects represent the culmination of their engineering education.

▸Fully autonomous robotic systems
▸Renewable & solar energy solutions
▸Biomedical & assistive technologies
▸AI-driven computer vision systems
▸Agricultural & environmental robotics
